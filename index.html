    • ABOUT US

    NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) in India dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged people through education, healthcare awareness, food distribution, and community development programs. The organization was founded with the vision of creating a better society by supporting vulnerable communities and empowering individuals to lead better lives. The foundation actively works in areas such as child education, women's empowerment, health awareness, menstrual hygiene, and social welfare activities. Through various campaigns and volunteer-driven initiatives, NayePankh Foundation provides essential support to people in need and promotes social responsibility among young individuals. The organization's mission is to ensure equal opportunities for education, health, and personal development for all sections of society. By engaging volunteers and community members, NayePankh Foundation continues to make a positive impact and contribute to sustainable social development across India. Through its dedication and service, NayePankh Foundation aims to build a society where every individual has access to basic necessities, quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth and success.
